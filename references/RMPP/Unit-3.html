Unit-3

Research Methods

Assignment Formative: Unit-3 Summary Post

Exploratory research seeks to comprehend a problem area and yields knowledge for more study (Stebbins, 2001). It is adaptable yet could not have a distinct direction. Contrarily, conclusive research uses quantitative approaches often in order to corroborate conclusions and hypotheses (Zikmund et al., 2013). It produces conclusive results but is not flexible. In order to explain phenomena from the viewpoint of the participant, qualitative research looks at phenomena in their natural surroundings (Denzin & Lincoln, 2011). It offers comprehensive, in-depth insights but might be biassed and has trouble extrapolating findings.
