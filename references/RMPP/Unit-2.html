Unit-2

Research Questions, the Literature Review and the Research Proposal

Assignment Formative: Unit-2 Peer Response 1
Assignment Formative: Unit-2 Peer Response 2

It's important to take into account the difficulties that come with crafting the appropriate queries. It calls for in-depth knowledge of the body of literature already published, critical thinking, inventiveness, and the capacity to balance wide and specific focuses (Stebbins, 2001). Furthermore, although asking the proper questions helps to concentrate, organise, and make sense of the research process, it does not ensure that the right answer will be discovered (Zikmund et al., 2013).
In conclusion, a competent researcher values the process of inquiry as least as highly as the outcome. A researcher may create a strong basis for meaningful, useful study by asking the appropriate questions.
