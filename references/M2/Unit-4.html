Unit-4

Breach Analysis and Mitigation

  • The reading this week focused on the issues around security breaches. Schwartz and Janger (2007) discuss the legal implications of security breaches. The articles below discussed the use of Kali Linux (and its associated tools) and provide a number of recommendations about their use. Bhatt (2018) sections 4 and 5 provided an overview of some of the most commonly used tools in Kali Linux and how to access them.

This week we used the techniques covered so far to review and evaluate security breaches and formulate responses to typical attacks. We also reviewed the suite of tools contained in the Kali Linux distribution that are focused on security assessments and testing
