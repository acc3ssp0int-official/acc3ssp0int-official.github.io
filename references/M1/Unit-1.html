Unit-1

Introduction to Cyber Security

Assignment: Unit-1 Initial Post

This week will focus on the drivers of computer-based information namely: types of networks, types of software, and databases; the internet and the World Wide Web and how it works. It discusses the various functions of these components in an information system and the impact of this on the system in the event of a security breach.

It begins by examining the considerable shift in paradigm from manual to digital approaches to processing and storing of data, and how this has consequently given rise to cybercrime and the impact of this on organisations and draws on the key concepts of CIA in Cyber Security, which serves as a background to the module
