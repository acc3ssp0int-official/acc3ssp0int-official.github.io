Unit-4

Threat Identification and Modelling

  • Review 3 threat modelling techniques that you have found are more commonly used in any industry of your choice
  • In deciding what techniques to apply, what considerations need to be made to professional, legal, social and ethical concerns?

In this unit, we will be working through exemplar problems where we will apply the knowledge gained in the lecturecast. It will involve working through a case to identify threats and vulnerabilities and apply threat modelling techniques to develop effective security measures to predict, detect, prevent or respond as discussed in unit 3.

The seminar this week provides you with practical examples and an opportunity to apply the knowledge gained in the lecturecast and reading to develop their technical skills. It ensures you are prepared to be able to work on the individual assignment for the module as part of Learning Outcome 2.
